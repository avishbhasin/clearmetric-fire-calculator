## Full Description

### Know exactly when you can retire — and what it takes to get there.

The ClearMetric FIRE Calculator Pro is a **professional-grade Excel spreadsheet** that tells you:

- **Your FIRE Number** — the exact portfolio size needed to cover your expenses forever
- **Whether you're on track** — gap/surplus analysis based on your current savings rate
- **When you'll actually reach FI** — even if it's different from your target
- **What to do about it** — specific action items to close any gap

### What's Inside

**Sheet 1: FIRE Calculator**
- Enter your age, income, expenses, savings, and investment allocation
- Instantly see your FIRE Number, projected corpus, and on-track status
- Coast FIRE number (when you can stop saving and let compounding do the work)
- Barista FIRE income needed (the part-time income to bridge the gap)
- Savings rate context: where you fall on the 10%→80% spectrum
- Color-coded status indicators (green = on track, red = shortfall)

**Sheet 2: Scenario Comparison**
- Compare 3 retirement scenarios side-by-side
- Scenario A links to your main inputs automatically
- Scenarios B & C: test aggressive and conservative paths
- See which strategy gets you to FIRE fastest

**Sheet 3: Year-by-Year Projection**
- 40 years of detailed projections
- Portfolio start, annual investment, growth, and portfolio end for every year
- FIRE Number tracking with % completion
- Beautiful line chart: portfolio growth vs. FIRE target
- Your retirement year highlighted in gold

**Sheet 4: How To Use**
- Complete guide to FIRE concepts (4% rule, Coast FIRE, Barista FIRE)
- Savings rate → years to FI reference table
- Investment allocation guidance
- Common FIRE milestones explained
